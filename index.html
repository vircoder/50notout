About us

‘Old Enough to know Better, Young Enough to do it Anyway’

At '50 Not Out' we dream to create a Family who make the above words come true in their lifetime. There are many folks out there who have beautifully scored a successful half century and are still managing the art of adding some spice and sparkle to their lives ahead. These high-spirited souls have known how to be good to oneself and others, to be lifelong learners, to share on every curve of their lives and, most importantly throughout this process, to keep that joyous and enthusiastic child alive within them no matter what challenges life has thrown in front of them.

We would like to reach out to all such brave and affectionate hearts - That Hey! You have certainly reached a milestone but the journey is going to get more interesting from here on. There are many promising things that await you. So come over, be a part of this family and lend your hand to make some difference in your life and others around and touch hearts across places.

Aashad Ka Pehla Badal - Starry Musical Night

Hello - Hope you are doing good. We are delighted to see the response for the 50 Not Out community and really appreciate efforts of all people who have motivated their parents/grandparents/in-laws to re-live their passion which was long lost in the journey of their life. Now, as this community has been specifically created to engage them and encourage them to socialise in their own age group, we never know, we might end up giving them the best gift - Their Time to make great friends :)

With all your love and blessings, we have planned an event in association with Dwarka Kala Sargam, where DKS members would be singing some of the evergreen melodies which anyone who is 50+ can relate to. About DKS, its a very active group of people who have their passion in Music and the age has never deterred them for being Young from their Heart. It would be fun to meet and mix-up with people there. Be there!!!
